Sung Jinwoo

The System Uses Me, and I Use the System

He is one of the awakened humans who became able to use mana, but was ranked as the World's Weakest Hunter, being given the ranking of weakest E-Rank Hunter. Following the traumatic experience of survival inside the Double Dungeon, Jinwoo became the "Player" of the System, a magical program that only he has access to that grants him unique overpowered abilities that no other hunters on Earth has. Now, Jinwoo soon delved down the path of becoming the World's Strongest Hunter after he was Reawakened, later revealed to be the System's purpose to make him into the next Shadow Monarch.
