Blood-Red Commander Igris

Igris is recognizable by his knightly appearance, including his crimson armor, black cape, and a distinctive long, red hair-like ornament on his helmet. He is known for his swordsmanship, stoic demeanor, and unwavering loyalty, quickly becoming a fan favorite.

Ant-King Beru

Beru is a powerful character, initially known as the Ant King, who serves as a major antagonist in the Jeju Island arc. He later becomes one of Sung Jinwoo's loyal shadow soldiers.

Kaisellin(Kaisel)

. After Sung Jinwoo defeats Baran, he turns Kaisellin into a shadow soldier and renames him Kaisel. Kaisel becomes one of Jinwoo's most loyal and versatile shadow soldiers, primarily used for transportation due to his speed and flight capabilities

Iron

Kim Chul, a member of the White Tiger Guild, was killed by Igris during a dungeon raid, and Jinwoo subsequently extracted his shadow. Iron retains Kim Chul's physical appearance, including his armor and weapons, but with a purple glow and enhanced abilities granted by Jinwoo's shadow magic.

Tank

loyal and powerful warrior in Sung Jinwoo's shadow army. He is a shadow bear, resembling a tank in size and strength, and is known for his resilience and ability to absorb damage. Initially, his name is unofficial, but he receives the official system-registered name "Tank" when he ranks up to Knight.
