Go Gunhee

A retired S-Rank hunter, he works as the chairman of the Korean Hunter Association before he died at the hand of the Frost Monarch, leaving his spot to his assistant, Woo Jinchul, as the new chairman of the association.

Baek Yoonho

Baek Yoonho (백윤호, Baek Yoon-ho) is a major supporting character in the Korean fantasy manhwa and light novel series, Solo Leveling. He is a Korean S-rank hunter and the leader of the White Tiger Guild.

Choi Jong-In

Choi Jong-In (Korean: 최종인) is a supporting character in the Korean action fantasy manhwa and light novel series, Solo Leveling. He is a S-rank hunter from Korea and the leader of the Hunters Guild.

Cha Hae-In

Cha Hae-In (Korean: 차해인) is one of the tritagonists in the dark fantasy Korean manhwa, light novel series Solo Leveling. She is an S-rank hunter and the co-leader of the guild, the Hunters Guild. She is the love interest-then-wife of the series' protagonist Sung Jinwoo and mother of Sung Suho.

Woo Jin-chul

Woo Jinchul (Korean: 우진철, U Jin-cheol; Akira Inukai in the Japanese version of the anime) is a major in the Korean fantasy manhwa and light novel series Solo Leveling. He is the former chief inspector of the surveillance team of the Korean Hunter Association, but eventually was promoted to as the new chairman of the assocation following the death of the original chairman, Go Gunhee.
